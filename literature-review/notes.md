# Aula 2 - Revisao bibliografica

## Passos principais

1. **Define your research question or topic**:
    - Before starting your literature revie w, you need to have a clear understanding of your research question or topic. This will help you to focus your search and identify relevant studies
2. **Conduct a comprehensive search**: 
    - Use appropriate databases, search engines, and other resources to find relevant studies. You may also want to consult reference lists, bibliographies, and citation indexes to identify additional studies.
3. **Evaluate the quality of the studies**:
    - Once you have identifies relevant studies, you need to evaluate their quality. This involves assessing their methodology, sample size, data analysis, and other factors that may affect the validity and reliability of their findings.
4. **Synthesize the findings**:
    - After evaluationg the studies, you need to synthesize their findings. This involves identifying patterns, trends, and themes in the literature and summarizing the key findings.
5. **Analyse and interpret the findings**: 
    - Once you have synthesized the literature, you need to analyze and interpret the findings.This involves identifying gaps in the literature, inconsistencies, and areas for future research.
6. **Write your literature review**: 
    - Finally, you need to write your literature review. This should be structured and organized in a logical and coherent way, with a clear introduction, body, and composition.


## Good Practices

- Develop a clear research question or topic
- Have a clear understanding of why is searching
- Use multiple databases
- Evaluate the quality of the studies
- Keep track of your sources
- Use a citation manager: Zotero, Endnote sws
- Be organized with the read literatures, then it will be possible to review these literatures again
